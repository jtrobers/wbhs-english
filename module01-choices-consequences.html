Module 01
Choices & Consequences

How do our choices shape who we become?

Module Overview

Students examine how choices shape lives and explore the consequences of decisions through Long Way Down and other texts that challenge characters to make difficult choices under pressure.

Essential Question: How do our choices shape who we become?
Making Claims Finding Evidence Explaining Reasoning Character Analysis Theme Development Discussion Symbolism

Formative Goal

Build in at least five formative activities for practice, feedback, participation, reading checks, and skill development.

Summative Goal

Plan for at least three summative opportunities across the course. This module can include one or more summative checks if needed.
